About Me

Where Every Dish
Tells a Story

Welcome to my little corner of the internet! I'm Saroja Poojary, and I've been cooking the traditional flavours of coastal Karnataka my entire life — recipes I learned from my mother, my grandmother, and the kitchens of Mangalore.

On my YouTube channel, I share authentic Tulu and Kannada recipes that are made with love, using locally sourced spices and time-honoured techniques. From everyday dals to festive feasts, every video is cooked with heart.

Whether you're a seasoned cook or a curious beginner, you'll find something delicious here. Pull up a chair — the food's almost ready!
